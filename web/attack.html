Attacks

☣️ Attack Info
- Deauth Attack: Closes the connection of WiFi devices by sending deauthentication frames to access points and client devices you selected.
- Beacon Attack: Beacon packets are used to advertise access points. By continuously sending beacon packets out, it will look like you created new WiFi networks.
- Probe Attack: Probe requests are sent by client devices to ask if a known network is nearby.
 In case of an unexpected error, please reload the site and look at the serial monitor for further debugging.
